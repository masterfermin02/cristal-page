Place the Creative Flow hero exports here:

- `hero-creative-flow.webm`
- `hero-creative-flow.mp4`

The hero component uses:

- `webm` as the preferred source
- `mp4` as fallback
- `/assets/img/hero/hero2.jpg` as the poster image

Recommended export settings:

- 1920x1080
- 6 to 10 seconds
- seamless loop
- no audio
